Sweet Balsamic Chicken

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Ingredients>

  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp rosemary (optional)

Description

Balsamic vineger is a very versatile ingredient that can be a healthy addition to a simple stovetop chicken recipe. We'll be balancing the tartness of the vinegar with honey and brown sugar for an easy and delicious meal.
